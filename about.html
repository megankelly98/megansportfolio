About Megan

My name is Megan Kelly and I am 22 years old. I come from a small village in south County Roscommon Ireland. I am currently studying Creative Digital Media in TU Dublin Blanchardstown. When I attend university, I live in Ongar in Dublin.

This is my portfolio website. It displays a lot of my work. I enjoy website design, photography, graphic design and my most recent hobbie, digital art. I only started doing digital art this year and it's really enjoyable!

Before studying Creative Digitial Media in TU Dublin, I did a PLC in Creative Media in Moate Business College in County Westmeath. During this time I got to go to Italy for three weeks on Erasmus+ and take part in work experience.
